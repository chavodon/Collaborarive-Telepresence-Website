Negative Connotations

Now even though there are many positive impacts yto be derived with the implementation of Collaborative Telepresence, there are still a few disadvantages associated with using and/or implementing such a technology. Some of these include but not limited to:

  1. The use of such technologies would be taxing on modern internet capabilities even with the introduction of 5G Networks.

  2. With the possible rise of this technology what will prevent users from choosing not to physically interact within the real world anymore?

  3. What safegaurds would be put in place to prevent system failure whether it be through hardware or network error?

  4. With the use of this technology other sectors may be greatly affected such as the transportation industry as individuals will be choosing to leave their homes less and less.
